# Why should I care?

### Your commit messages are documentation whether you like it or not
    - Every line of code you write gets annotated by a commit message.  Make the most of the opportunity.
    - It's possible to give AI tools the commit history also.  A little extra context can yield better results.

### AI tools deserve their own sandbox
    - AI tools can get a lot done when you give them broad prompts, but they can also run amok in your codebase.
    - When AI work is isolated, you can afford to give it the latitude it works best with, and then take the best of what it produces into your final changes.
